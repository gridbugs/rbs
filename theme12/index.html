CSE Revue is an annual sketch comedy show incorporating dancing, singing, sketches, videos and all things funny! With our roots in Computer Science and Engineering, the show delivers a fresh, innovative take on comedy.

Since 2002, CSE Revue has proven to be a hit with a wide range of audiences, leaving everyone awed by the special effects, slick videos, exciting set design and our commitment to make them laugh.

This year, CSE Revue is busting bad code! So relax, grab a bunch of friends and come for an all round good night! If there's something strange about your sense of humour, give us a call (or an email) and we'll save you from a comedy-less night.
